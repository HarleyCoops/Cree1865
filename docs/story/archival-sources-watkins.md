## Watkins' own handwriting — NOT freely digitized

The richest prize — 67 items (journals 1852-63, letters, including refs to
Cree dictionary work) — is held at:

- **University of Birmingham, Cadbury Research Library (CMS Archive)**
- Finding number: CMS/B/OMS/C/C1/O71/1-67
- Catalog: https://calmview.bham.ac.uk/Record.aspx?src=CalmView.Catalog&id=XCMS%2FB%2F1803-1880%2FC1%2FO%2F10-75%2F71
- Scans exist in Adam Matthew Digital "Church Missionary Society Archive" Part 2
  (North-West Canada) but that is **subscription-only**. Originals viewable in
  person at Birmingham.
